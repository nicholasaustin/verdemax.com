About VerdeMax

VerdeMax is a holding company built to acquire and operate essential service businesses with a long-term perspective. We focus on Texas — Houston and Dallas — where growing markets, strong demand, and owner-operated businesses create real opportunity.

Our model is straightforward: acquire a business with solid cash flow, support the seller through a clean transition, place a capable operator, and build durable value over years — not months. We're not financial engineers or flippers. We're operators who take the long view.

How We Work

A straightforward process — no games, no surprises.

1. Introduction

15–30 minutes. We learn about your business, goals, and timeline. No pressure, no obligation.

2. NDA & Review

We sign a mutual NDA and review basic financials. If there's a fit, we move to a Letter of Intent within two weeks.

3. Close & Transition

We work with you through due diligence and closing. Seller involvement in transition is welcomed — we want a clean handoff.
